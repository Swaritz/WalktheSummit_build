Projects

Current project

We are proud to support the Himalayan International School, situated near the scenic Kempty Falls, just 12 km from Mussoorie
To begin with, we have established a reliable WiFi connection and installed a
state-of-the-art 50-inch LED TV, granting students seamless access to a vast array of online educational resources with teaching tools
Additionally, we are providing comprehensive stationery kits to ensure that students have all the necessary tools.
-
We are also giving a laptop to the school, which will assist teachers in preparing lessons and enable students to know world of digital learning.
We have also planned a variety of activities and sports matches to foster a sense of community, creativity, and friendly competition.
Our ultimate goal is to create a holistic environment that nurtures the students' intellectual, physical, and emotional development, setting them up for success in all aspects of life.
