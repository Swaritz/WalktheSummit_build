About Me

My experience

I am a Minecraft Mod Developer, with 9 mods for Minecraft: Java Edition and over 85k downloads across both CurseForge and Modrinth.
-
My favorite mod that I've really enjoyed working on would be Health Indicators, which displays health bars over entities.
-
Other than that, Automatic Infinite Elytra is probably my biggest + most feature rich mod, with an Elytra Autopilot, Auto Area Explorer, and even a music player (for long journeys).
-
My most popular project, however, would be Farlands Reborn, which is also my first ever mod.
